What's Inside

Everything You Need to
Understand Dining Sentiment

Built with real data, real NLP, and real insights — not mock numbers.

🧠

VADER Sentiment NLP

Every review is scored with NLTK's VADER — a lexicon-based model tuned for social text, giving positive, neutral, and negative classifications with a confidence score.

🗺️

Interactive 3D Map

Pydeck-powered 3D column map of all Bangalore restaurant locations, colour-coded by sentiment health. Hover any pin to see review stats instantly.

☁️

Positive & Negative Word Clouds

Visual summaries of what customers love (green) and complain about (red) — per restaurant, dynamically generated from actual review text.

🔍

Live Review Analyzer

Paste any review text and get an instant sentiment result with a visual gauge, score, and confidence percentage — powered by the same NLP engine.

🏆

Restaurant Health Leaderboard

Composite health scores ranking all 6,926 restaurants by sentiment quality — with filters by area, minimum reviews, and CSV export.

📍

90-Location Analysis

Discover which Bangalore neighbourhoods have the happiest diners — ranked charts with a review volume vs. sentiment quality scatter plot.
